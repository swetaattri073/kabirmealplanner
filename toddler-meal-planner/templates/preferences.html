💡 Tips for Picky Eaters

🔄 Keep Trying

It can take 10-15 exposures for a toddler to accept a new food. Don't give up after a few refusals!

🎨 Make it Fun

Cut foods into fun shapes, arrange colorfully on the plate, or let them help in the kitchen.

🍲 Mix with Favorites

Hide vegetables in dal, paratha, or khichdi. Blend spinach into paneer dishes.

👨‍👩‍👧 Eat Together

Toddlers learn by watching. Eat the same foods together and show enthusiasm.

⏰ Timing Matters

Introduce new foods when hungry but not starving. Mid-morning or early afternoon works well.

🚫 No Pressure

Avoid forcing or bribing. Let them explore at their own pace. Praise trying, not finishing.
