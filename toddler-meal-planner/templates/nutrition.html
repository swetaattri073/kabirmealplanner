🇮🇳 Key Nutrients for Indian Toddlers

🩸

Iron

Indian vegetarian diets can be low in iron. Include ragi, spinach, dates, and pair with vitamin C foods for absorption.

🐟

Omega-3

Supports brain and eye development. Breast milk / DHA-fortified formula, fatty fish (deboned), and ground walnuts or flax help meet needs.

☀️

Vitamin D

Most Indian children are Vitamin D deficient. Morning sunlight (10-15 mins before 10am) is the best source.

🔴

Vitamin B12

Found mainly in animal products. Vegetarian toddlers should have milk, curd, paneer daily or consider supplements.

💪

Protein

Combine dal + rice for complete protein. Include paneer, curd, eggs (if non-veg) regularly.
